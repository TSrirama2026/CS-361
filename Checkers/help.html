Rules

  1. Both sides will start with 12 pawns


  2. Checkers can only move diagonally forwards, unless you promote a pawn to a king, in which case, it can also move backwards


  3. Players will alternate turns after every move


  4. If you can eat another player's pawn, you MUST do so


  5. You can eat another player's pawn if there is an open space immediadtely beyond it, and continue eating more pawns on that same turn until there are no more left


  6. A player wins when the opponenet's pawns are all taken off the table, or when the opponent can't make any more moves
